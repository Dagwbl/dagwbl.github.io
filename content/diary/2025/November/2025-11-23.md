##  How to give a presentation

### Introduction

Hello everyone, my name is Jinpeng,
and today I'm going to talk about *topic* 
	- going to give a presentation about/on *topic*
	- going to share about *topic*

The presentation will be in *# parts*
	First... Second... Third...
The presentation will be cover *subtopic 1, subtopic 2... and subtopic 3*.

\* Please feel free to ask questions during the presentation
or please hold your questions until the end of the presentation


### Sequence/Transition words

First, I'd like to talk about *subtopic 1*
	Why is this important?
	What do we need to know?
In conclusion/In sum/To conclude, main point about *subtopic 1* (to reiterate, to say something again)
...
Okay, now I'd like to move on to *subtopic 2*.
Lastly, I'd like to talk about *subtopic 3*.
- First, second, third, fourth...
- Next, then, after that, last/lastly...
- Additionally, moreover, furthermore...

### Conclusions and Q&A

I'd like to conclude today's presentation by summarizing the information.

To end today's presentation...

Q&A: If there are any questions, I'd be happy to address them.
